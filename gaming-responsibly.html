Controlling addiction

To begin with, are you addicted? It’s always a difficult question to answer. If you’re not sure, take this quick, anonymous test and discover what the findings indicate. Please keep in mind that until you take an honest look at yourself, it is very difficult to identify whether or not you have an addiction.

If you think you may have a gambling issue, you should take the necessary steps to suspend or remove your account until you find a solution. Instead of suspending or canceling your account, if you would rather better manage your gaming habits and expenditures, you can choose to impose limits on your deposits, losses, wagers, and session durations.

We provide you numerous options for limiting your playtime. Limits placed on your account or bets cannot be removed for your own protection. You must wait at least 24 hours after requesting the lowering of the restriction before you may do so. The rise in limitations will have immediate impact. Go to the “Settings” page of your profile to set the following restrictions.

Deposit Cap

Once you’ve selected the deposit limit, you won’t be able to make additional deposits. It is possible to set the deposit cap for a daily, weekly, or monthly duration. The limit is immediately effective.

  • Loss Limit

    When you set a loss limit, you cannot lose more than that amount. It is possible to set the loss cap on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis. If your limit is reached, you won’t be able to play for real money for a set period of time. The limit is immediately effective.

  • Betting cap

    Limit the total amount of money you are willing to risk for a predetermined period of time. Your bet limit has been reached, and you are not able to place any more bets when it has been reached. You will be notified when this happens. The limit is immediately effective.

  • Session limit

    You can change the time limit for your game session. The clock on the website will start to count down as soon as you log in. When the timer goes out, you’ll be informed that your session limit has been reached and given the option to log out or continue playing.

  • Cool-down period

    Furthermore, you are in charge of the cool-off period for your account. During the cool-off period, you won’t be able to play for real money or make deposits to the bookmaker. The configuration is immediately effective. When your account expires, it cannot be automatically reinstated. Please go to the “Settings” page for your profile and click “Apply”.

Self-exclusion

You have the option to set a self-exclusion length for your player account, which can range from 24 hours to two months. Please contact the Customer Care department if you need an extended period of time or even a permanent ban. The configuration is immediately effective. There will be a time when you are unable to access your account due to self-exclusion. It is not possible for you both to change the self-exclusion time. Make sure you request the cashout before setting up the self-exclusion; if you don’t, you won’t be able to request a withdrawal.

We’ll help you make use of the self-exclusion function. You understand, however, that we shall have no duty or liability whatsoever in the event that we are unable to determine or recognize that you are continuing to gamble or attempt to use the Website.

How to avoid developing a gambling addiction

If you follow the rules given below, you can play the game without experiencing any anxiety:

  • It’s fun to play the slots. Never think of it as a way to get money; Avoid trying to “recover” your losses by limiting the amount of money and time you can spend gaming. Observe these limitations;
  • Make frequent breaks. Once the maximum limit has been established, don’t raise it;
  • Never borrow money to place a wager; If you believe you won’t be able to stop in time, never be hesitant to ask for help;
  • Try to keep up a full social schedule, spend more time with your family, and pursue new hobbies.
  • You should only start playing when you are relaxed and concentrated. Do not gamble if you are under the influence of alcohol or any other drug.
  • If you feel that you are unable to manage your gambling, get help.
