Legality of 888starz in Bangladesh

In 2018, an alliance between the Tron Foundation and the 888starz team using the Tron blockchain resulted in the 888Tron project. This project is based on the idea of creating a transparent mechanism for rewarding and motivating customers of the gaming platform, the so-called “Reward Model”. Within the framework of this project, users who are active on the platform receive 888 tokens as a reward, which brings them additional income, even passive. The token is given to all players as a reward, regardless of whether the bet is won or lost. The player can withdraw their tokens at any time to their cryptocurrency wallet or sell them on a crypto exchange. This reward model was so well-liked by users that after 2 months of the project, more than 40% of all Tron blockchain transactions were accounted for by the 888Tron project. As a result, the project has been repeatedly recognised as the fastest-growing and developing crypto project.

To develop their crypto project even further, the 888Tron team has acquired the online gaming platform 888starz. It is here that users can get a first-class sports betting and casino experience, and make transactions with over 35 cryptocurrencies, or use 888 tokens as a payment method on the platform.

The 888starz platform, which started operations in 2020, has been granted a Curaçao gambling licence No. 8048/JAZ2020-048. The licence acts as a guarantee of the platform’s reliability and safety. In addition, this Curaçao licence ensures that 888starz can legally operate in the countries where it provides its services, including Bangladesh.
